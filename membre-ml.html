PRÉSENTATION

I was a Ph.D. Student in Computer Science at University Paris Descartes. My research topic concerns the Data series management and analytics. In a world where complex Big Data Analysis need to deal with Data Sequences, we study and research the possibility to provide new smart techniques for extracting knowledge from this kind of data. Our goal is clear and well defined: provide fast Data Driven discovery which is feasible only if fast query techniques are available.
